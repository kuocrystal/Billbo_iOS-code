Help Center


Q: Only the first receipt on my submission was processed. What happened?

There's a really simple (and easily fixable) explanation for this. You probably submitted a PDF with multiple documents in it. If so, only the first document will be processed by our system since we are unable to split PDFs into multiple entries.


Q: Can I use Billbo from anywhere in the world?

We are going to be honest with you, you can use Billbo from anywhere in the world!


Q: Does Billbo scan and process receipts in other currencies?

The short answer is "YES!" Billbo currently supports the following currencies: USD, AUD, CAD, NZD, MXN, EUR, JPY, GBP, DKK kr, PLN, SEK kr, CHF, HKD, NTD, and SGD.
