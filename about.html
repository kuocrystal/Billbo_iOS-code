About Billbo


3 Simple steps: Scan, Tap and Check

Billbo is an app for keeping track of your receipts. It's a cloud-based app, you can use it through a web browser. Tracker is available for iOS and Android!


Billbo is simplistic in the way that it allows you to photograph and tag receipts. You can always check all receipts anytime you want! Most importantly, Billbo has a clean interface that is easy to use!
